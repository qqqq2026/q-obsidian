## Proposed Experiments (shortlisted 2026-03-12)

### RL-002 — AI Voice Receptionist
- **Stage:** Proposed
- **Hypothesis:** Bundle DID Services phone numbers with AI voice agents. Sell to SMBs as 24/7 AI receptionist (dental, legal, trades, real estate).
- **Cost to run:** €10–30/month per client (API costs)
- **Price point:** €99–299/month per client
- **Margin:** 85–90%
- **Time to first revenue:** 2–3 weeks
- **Why strong:** D. already owns DID infrastructure — no cold start
- **Status:** Awaiting D. green light

### RL-003 — Productized AI Automation Agency
- **Stage:** Proposed
- **Hypothesis:** Fixed-scope AI automation packages for SMBs at €500–1,500/month retainer. Q delivers, D.'s consulting network = warm pipeline.
- **Cost to run:** ~€20–50/month (API costs only)
- **Price point:** €500–1,500/month retainer
- **Margin:** 90%+
- **Time to first revenue:** 1–2 weeks
- **Why strong:** Zero product build. Fastest path to cash.
- **Status:** Awaiting D. green light

### RL-004 — Niche AI SaaS (micro-tool)
- **Stage:** Proposed
- **Hypothesis:** Build a single-purpose AI tool for a vertical D. has access to (consulting, telecom). Self-serve at €29–99/month.
- **Cost to build:** Q builds MVP in 3–5 days
- **Price point:** €29–99/month
- **Margin:** 95%+
- **Time to first revenue:** 3–4 weeks
- **Why strong:** Compounds over time, low churn, no sales calls at scale
- **Status:** Awaiting D. to pick vertical/niche
